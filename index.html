MARKETING COMUNICATION MANAGER

(SOUTH KOREA MARKET)

I was responsible for all forms of marketing and sales in South Korea, including managing the launch of a website in the market. One of my key contributions included a business plan for the establishment of a physical retail location, starting with a pop-up location in Seoul, given that the Korean market still has a considerable offline presence and that a physical location would help build trust and brand loyalty for those ordering online.

Taking into account our disadvantage in knowledge and expertise compared to local retailers, I decided to subcontract the operation of our physical location to a well-established local department store. I selected and negotiated with Shinsaegae and Lotte, the two largest Korean department store chains, and consulted with our CEO throughout the process.
